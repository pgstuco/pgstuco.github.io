Student Council Retreat

July 04, 2017

On July 3rd, 2017, members of the 2017-2018 Point Grey Student Council gathered around the sun-baked docks of Granville Island for the Student Council Retreat. While the focus of the trip was to strengthen connections between council members and to establish a common vision, the student council participated in a wide range of activities, varying from scavenger hunts to group discussions. They also had the honor of being joined by past PG alumni who provided insight and advice on his experience as a leader who is involved with student societies. Without a doubt, the PG Student Council is excited put together a fun year for our fellow Greyhounds!
